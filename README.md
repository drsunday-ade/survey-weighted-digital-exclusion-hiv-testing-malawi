# malawi-hiv-digital-gap

Survey-weighted analysis of the 2024 Malawi Demographic and Health Survey examining whether internet use is associated with ever HIV testing among women aged 15–49 years, with emphasis on digital exclusion, inequality gradients, and rural–urban heterogeneity.

## Overview
This repository contains reproducible code, analytic outputs, and manuscript-supporting materials for a nationally representative study of digital exclusion and HIV-testing inequality among women in Malawi.

## Study title
**Digital exclusion and HIV-testing inequality among women in Malawi: a nationally representative study using the 2024 Demographic and Health Survey**
